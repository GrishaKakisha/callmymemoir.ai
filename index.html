MemoirAI
Preserve your life stories with a phone call.

MemoirAI helps older adults preserve their life stories as a written memoir their family can read and share for generations. No apps, no screens, no typing — just a phone call.

For organizations

MemoirAI is available as a group program for senior living communities, insurance wellness programs, and other organizations that want to offer meaningful, low-friction legacy work to the people they serve.

Participants call a dedicated phone number provided by their community or organization, tell their stories at their own pace, and receive a completed memoir they can share with their family.

Organizations receive only anonymized participation metrics — no story content, transcripts, or personal information. We believe the people we serve should always own their stories.

To discuss bringing MemoirAI to your community, contact magpie.ai.llc@gmail.com.

Privacy is the product

The stories people share with MemoirAI belong to them — not to us, and not to any organization that provides the service. We never sell personal data. We never use stories for advertising or marketing.

Organizations that offer MemoirAI to their members receive only anonymized, aggregate usage statistics — never the content of any call, transcript, or memoir.

For the legal specifics, see our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

How calls and messaging work

At the start of each call, participants are informed that they are speaking with an AI assistant, that the call is recorded to create their memoir, and that a text message will be sent to their phone number when the memoir is ready. Message and data rates may apply. Participants may reply STOP to any message to unsubscribe, or HELP for assistance. By continuing the call after this disclosure, the caller consents to the recording and to receiving the memoir delivery message.

All calls are recorded and retained as evidence of verbal consent. Full terms are in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.
