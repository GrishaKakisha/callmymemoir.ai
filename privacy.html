MemoirAI

Privacy Policy

Effective: March 30, 2026

MemoirAI ("we," "us," "our") provides a phone-based service that helps people preserve their life stories as written memoirs. This Privacy Policy describes what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you use MemoirAI, we collect:

  • Phone number — captured automatically when you call our service. Used to identify you on return calls and to send you an SMS when your memoir is ready.
  • Call recordings and transcripts — your conversations with our AI assistant are recorded and transcribed to create your memoir.
  • Name, birthday, and birthplace — if you share these during the conversation, we store them to personalize your memoir.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to provide the MemoirAI service:

  • To recognize you when you call back and recall your previous stories
  • To create a written memoir from your conversations
  • To send you a single SMS notification with a link to your completed memoir

We do not sell your information. We do not use your stories or personal information for advertising or marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

To provide our service, we work with the following third-party providers. These providers process your data solely to deliver the MemoirAI service:

  • Vapi — handles phone calls, voice recognition, and transcription
  • Anthropic — provides the AI that synthesizes your stories into a written memoir
  • Twilio — delivers SMS notifications to your phone
  • Railway — hosts our application and database

We do not share your information with any other third parties.

Data Retention

Your stories, transcripts, and memoirs are stored indefinitely so that you can continue adding to your memoir over time. If you would like your data deleted, contact us and we will remove all of your information from our systems.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted connections (HTTPS) for all data in transit and access controls on our database. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may:

  • Request a copy of the data we have about you
  • Request that we delete all of your data
  • Opt out of SMS messages by replying STOP to any message from us

Children's Privacy

MemoirAI is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
